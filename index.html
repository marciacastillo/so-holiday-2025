This year’s holiday card was inspired by the children from South America who celebrated our Divestreams with art, creativity, and enthusiasm. We were delighted to see their inner scientists come alive. Explore the gifts on this imaginary seafloor, recreated in a child’s room, using ROV SuBastian. Each gift represents one of the year’s seven expeditions and shares a highlight to celebrate.
